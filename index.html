Required Notice - Please Read Carefully

What is "move-in ready" room condition?

A move-in ready room is a room that does not require any further cleaning or preparation after you move out. Since your room is furnished, it must be left in a condition where the next tenant can move in immediately.

If, after inspection, a housekeeper needs to enter the room to prepare it for the next tenant, a $150 cleaning fee will be deducted from your deposit.

If you choose to clean the room yourself, we strongly recommend cleaning it thoroughly, placing new or fresh bed linens, taking clear photos afterward, and sending them to us to help avoid any cleaning-related charges.
